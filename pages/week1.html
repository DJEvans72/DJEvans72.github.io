Week 1 - Basics

This week is all about the basics. So often its something we forget to teach, yet expect every one to be able to. Also its good to remind ourselves of the basics when there are so many other things to remember when coaching and playing.

In case your new to the site, each week we focus on a batting, fielding and bowling skill and give one thing to think about for each game to focus on in each area of the game

Taking Guard

Ever wondered what taking guard is? this video shows you how to do it. Taking guard is allows you as a batsman to identify where you are in relation to the stumps you are defending. It's done by asking the umpire at the other end of the wicket to tell you whether you have aligned your bat with the stumps or not.

Where to stand and why

Great, so you;ve taken guard and you know where your middle stump is. Do I always line my stance up in line with middle stump? This great video takes us inside the mind of one of Englands best batsmen, as Mark Ramprakash tells us shows he adapts his alignment to the stumps for different conditions and types of bowling.
